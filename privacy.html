Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Pantry Tracker ("the App") helps you track food expiration dates and reduce waste. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

What We Collect

  • Food items you add: Names, expiration dates, locations, and notes
  • App settings: Notification preferences, quiet hours
  • Usage history: When items are added, used, or tossed (stored locally)

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Analytics or usage tracking
  • Advertising identifiers

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device.

  • Your food items and history are stored in a local SQLite database
  • Settings are stored in device storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No cloud sync or account required

Data Export

  • You may export your data as a JSON backup file at any time
  • Export is user-initiated through Settings → Export Backup
  • The exported file is saved to your device or shared via your chosen method
  • We have no access to your exported data

Notifications

  • The App uses local notifications to remind you about expiring food
  • Notifications are scheduled on-device
  • No push notification servers are involved
  • You can disable notifications at any time in Settings

Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage providers

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with anyone.

Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App contains no features that target children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact:

Email: support@ndycode.com

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You have full control over your data
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the App
  • You can export your data at any time
  • No request to us is needed to access or delete your data
